About the Artist

Veronika Mazitova

A professional artist and designer with over 20 years of experience across fine art and commercial illustration. Based in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, working under the brand MazitovaArt.

My work spans acrylic and gouache painting, portraiture, digital illustration in Procreate, luminescent painting, and logo design. Each piece carries warmth, atmosphere, and a touch of the magical — rooted in the rich visual heritage of Kyrgyzstan.

I also teach drawing to children, nurturing creativity in the next generation of artists.
